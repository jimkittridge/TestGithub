Durham continues to be one of the fastest-growing markets in North Carolina for small warehouse and flex space tenants. Whether you're a contractor looking for bay space, a service business that needs warehouse with office, or an e-commerce operator that needs fulfillment space, Durham's position in the Research Triangle offers advantages that are hard to match.

## Why tenants choose Durham

The Research Triangle gives businesses access to a large and growing customer base, strong connectivity for deliveries and service calls, and proximity to RTP, Duke University, and RDU Airport. For contractors, trades, and light industrial operators, that means more jobs, shorter drive times, and a professional base of operations.

## What kind of space is available

Durham flex space and small warehouse suites typically range from 950 to 3,000 SF, with drive-in doors, 14–20 ft clear height, and 24/7 access. These are built for businesses that need functional industrial space without committing to a big-box warehouse lease.

## Who it's a good fit for

- Contractors and trades (electricians, plumbers, HVAC, painters, landscapers)
- Service businesses that need warehouse plus office
- E-commerce and fulfillment operators
- Light manufacturing and assembly
- Biotech and scientific support businesses serving RTP
