Columbia sits at the intersection of three major interstates — I-26, I-77, and I-20 — making it one of the best-connected logistics points in the Southeast. For businesses that need small warehouse space, flex industrial suites, or contractor bay space, Columbia offers reach, affordability, and a growing customer base.

## Why Columbia is a strong market

As South Carolina's capital, Columbia combines a stable economy with affordable operating costs. The metro has attracted steady industrial and distribution investment, and demand for small bay flex space continues to grow from:

- Contractors and trades serving the Midlands
- Distribution and fulfillment operators leveraging the interstate corridor
- Service businesses that need warehouse with office
- Government and military support contractors near Fort Jackson

## What's available in Columbia

Small warehouse and flex suites in Columbia range from 2,000 to 4,000 SF, with:

- Drive-in doors for loading and vehicle access
- 14–18 ft clear height
- 200A power
- HVAC in office areas
- 24/7 access
- 1–3 year lease terms

## Reach from Columbia

From a Columbia base, businesses can reach Charlotte, Charleston, Atlanta, and Savannah within a few hours. Local submarkets include Cayce/West Columbia, Northeast Columbia, Irmo, Forest Acres, and the Lexington corridor.
