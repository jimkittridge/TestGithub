For many service businesses, the ideal space isn't purely office or purely warehouse — it's both. A small warehouse with office gives you room for inventory, tools, and equipment alongside a front office, back office, or customer-facing area.

## What makes a good warehouse-with-office setup

The best flex space and small warehouse suites are designed to support both functions without compromise:

- **Warehouse area** with drive-in door access, clear height for racking, and enough floor space for storage or staging
- **Office area** with HVAC, lighting, and a layout suitable for admin work, customer meetings, or a reception area
- **Shared utilities** including power (100–200 amps), 24/7 keycard access, and dedicated parking

## Who needs this type of space

- Service businesses (plumbing, HVAC, electrical, cleaning, landscaping)
- Contractors who need a dispatch base plus admin space
- E-commerce operators who need packing/shipping plus order management
- Small distributors who need warehouse plus a small sales office

## What to check before signing a lease

1. **Drive-in door size** — Can your vehicles and deliveries fit?
2. **Clear height** — Enough room for racking and vertical storage?
3. **Power** — Sufficient amps for your tools and equipment?
4. **HVAC** — Is the office area climate-controlled?
5. **Lease terms** — Flexible enough for your business stage?
6. **24/7 access** — Can you get in when you need to?
