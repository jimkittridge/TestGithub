The greater Asheville area — and Fletcher in particular — has become one of the strongest markets in Western North Carolina for businesses looking for flex space, small warehouse units, and contractor bay space.

## Why Fletcher works for flex and warehouse tenants

Fletcher sits just south of Asheville with direct access to I-26 and I-40, plus Asheville Regional Airport (AVL) just five minutes away. For contractors, service businesses, and light industrial operators, this means:

- Quick access to job sites across Western NC
- Convenient dispatch for crews covering Asheville, Hendersonville, and surrounding areas
- A functional base with real industrial specs, not a converted retail or office space

## What's available

Flex space and small warehouse suites near Asheville typically range from 1,000 to 3,000 SF. Standard features include:

- 10' × 14' drive-in doors
- 14–20 ft clear height
- 200A power
- HVAC in office areas
- 24/7 keycard access
- 1–3 year lease terms

## Who leases this type of space

- Contractors and trades working across Western NC
- Service businesses with combined warehouse and office needs
- E-commerce and fulfillment operators
- Light manufacturing and assembly businesses
- Creative and industrial makers
