Our Story

GetFlexSpace was founded on a simple observation: small businesses in the Carolinas were being underserved by the commercial real estate market. Traditional warehouse leases required too much space, too much commitment, and came with too little flexibility.

We set out to change that. Our small bay flex spaces—ranging from 1,000 to 3,000 square feet—are purpose-built for the businesses that power local economies: HVAC contractors, e-commerce operators, craft brewers, fitness studios, and creative professionals.

What Sets Us Apart

Right-Sized Spaces

No more leasing 10,000 sq ft when you only need 2,000. Our suites are designed for small businesses that need room to operate without the overhead of a massive warehouse.

Professional Grade

High ceilings, drive-in doors, 3-phase power, and full HVAC. These aren't converted strip mall units—they're built from the ground up for industrial and flex use.

Hands-On Management

Responsive maintenance, clean common areas, and a leasing team that actually picks up the phone. We treat our tenants like partners, not account numbers.

Growing Footprint

With locations in Asheville, Durham, and Columbia, we're expanding across the Carolinas to meet the surging demand for small bay industrial space.
